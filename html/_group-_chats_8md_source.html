1 Group chats.
2 
3 Note: we assume everyone in the chat trusts each other.
4 
5 These group chats work by temporarily adding the 4 "closest" people defined by a distance function
6 in group.c in order to form a circle of connected peers. These peers then relay messages to each other.
7 
8 A friend invites another friend to a group chat by sending them an invite packet. The friend either ignores
9 the invite or responds with a response packet if he wants to join the chat. The friend invite contains the type
10 of groupchat (text only, A/V) the friend is being invited to.
